Last updated: March 2026

The app may collect the following data: • Car photos you optionally upload (stored on Supabase Storage) • When signing in with Google: your email address and profile picture (via Firebase Authentication) • Model car information you add to your collection (brand, series, year, condition, notes) • Community data: Posts you share, likes, and comments you write • Social graph: Users you follow and your followers • Your device's camera (used only when you want to take a photo)

2. How We Use Your Data

Collected data is used only for: • Cloud backup and synchronization across devices • Managing the community feed and interactions • AI-powered content moderation (Groq AI) Your data is never sold or used for third-party advertising.

3. Cloud Services and AI Moderation

The app uses Google Firebase and Supabase: • Firebase Firestore: Secure cloud storage for collection and social data. • Supabase Storage: Secure cloud storage for photos. • AI Moderation: Comments and captions are automatically moderated by Groq AI for community safety. Only text content is analyzed; no personal metadata (IDs, names) is transmitted to the AI service. Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy Supabase Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

4. Data Deletion and Control

• **Comment Deletion**: You can delete your comments at any time by long-pressing on the comment within the app. • **Post Deletion**: You can remove your shared posts via your profile page. • **Account Deletion**: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (collection, social graph, account) via the "Delete Account" button on the "Profile" page. Alternatively, email taytekofficial@gmail.com.

5. Minors and account use

BaseHW is built for adults and teens who can lawfully agree to our terms. We do not promote the app to children under 13 or aim to gather their personal data on purpose. If you are a parent or guardian and think we may hold information about a child by mistake, email us; we will review the request and delete qualifying data.

We shape our systems around safeguards that fit the kinds of data we process. Any online service carries residual risk: networks and devices can be attacked or misconfigured.
